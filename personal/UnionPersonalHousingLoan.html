PERSONAL LOAN FOR EXISTING HOUSING LOAN BORROWERS

PURPOSE

For house related miscellaneous expenses such as housing warming, furniture, interiors, payment of insurance premium etc.

ELIGIBILITY

To avail "Union Personal for Housing Loan Borrowers" you need to meet the following criteria:
Salaried or non-salaried borrowers, who have availed housing loan from our Bank, which is in standard category.

QUANTUM OF LOAN

5% of Housing loan amount, subject to a maximum Rs. 5 lakh

MARGIN, I.E. YOUR SHARE

Nil

MORATORIUM PERIOD

Nil

REPAYMENT

Maximum repayment tenure is to be the earlier of the following :
  • Repayment period of up to 5 years
  • Residual tenure of Housing Loan

RATE OF INTEREST AND PROCESSING CHARGES

  •   To know rate of interest, please click here

  • No Processing Charges.

PREPAYMENT PENALTY

  • There is NO prepayment penalty if the loan is adjusted by the borrower from his own verifiable legitimate sources

  • A takeover charges of 2% on the average outstanding for balance of the preceding 12 months, if the loan is closed on taken over by any other Bank /FI.

GUARANTEE

  • Guarantee of the spouse required.
  • In case borrower is unmarried/widow/widower, 3rd party guarantee of sufficient means required.

SECURITY

An undertaking from the borrower that he/she will adjust the personal loan account prior to adjusting the home loan account.
