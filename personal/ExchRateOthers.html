EXCHANGE RATE FEED OVER REUTERS

RET_AD

RET_AD is the source to receive live feed of exchange rates on various currencies from Reuters on an ongoing basis to the bank

Features of RET_AD
  • Availability of on line real time exchange market rates.
  • No loss of time in telephonic conversation
  • Take advantage of intra-day volatility.
  • Full transparency in exchange rates.
  • Highly competitive exchange margins.

RET_AD enables the “B” Category branches to provide fast, transparent seamless and uninterrupted services and quote highly competitive exchange rate to customers
