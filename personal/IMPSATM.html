IMPS Fund Transfer through ATM

IMPS fund transfer can also be initiated at any of our ATMs. This facility is available 24x7 and 365 days a year.   Please follow the steps as under for initiating IMPS fund transfer through ATM:

  • Visit any Union Bank ATM
  • Select “ Fund Transfer” option from the main menu
  • Further select “ IMPS Fund Transfer”
  • ATM fetches your registered mobile number on the screen and displays it.
  • Enter  beneficiary’s account mobile number
  • Enter beneficiary’s account MMID
  • Enter the amount for fund transfer
  • Confirm the IMPS Fund  transfer details
  • Upon confirmation amount is transferred to the beneficiary on a real time basis
  • Both remitter and beneficiary get notifications for the transaction on their mobile.

Fund Transfer Limit:
Daily fund transfer limit is Rs.50,000/-.
