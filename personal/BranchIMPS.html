Branch IMPS

Union Bank is the first Bank in the Industry to launch IMPS through branches for its Retail and Corporate customers by integrating IMPS platform in its CBS (Core Banking Solution). Under this facility customers can approach any of our branches and request for instant fund transfer. IMPS facility allows customers to instantly transfer funds to any other bank account up to Rs. 2 lacs per day .All that one needs to do is key in beneficiary's valid account number and IFSC code.
