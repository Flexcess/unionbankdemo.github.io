Deposit Schemes :

  • Current accounts in HKD, and major Foreign Currencies by individuals either singly or in joint names / Partnership concerns / Limited companies / Societies / Proprietorship concerns.

UNION BANKOF INDIA, Hong Kong is a member of the Deposit Protection Scheme. Eligible deposits taken by this Bank are protected by the Scheme up to a limit of HK$500,000 per depositor.
