Purpose

Union Saubhagya provides hassle free finance to micro depositors in case of urgent credit needs.

Eligibility

To avail the loan, the smart card holder should have transacted for a period of one month and accumulated minimum of Rs. 500/- as deposit.

Quantum of loan

Two times of amount of deposit will be considered as loan amount up-to maximum of Rs.10000 in multiples of Rs.1000.

Margin

Nil

Security

Nil

Rate Of Interest

Please click here to know our latest rate of interest.

Repayment

Bullet repayment period for short-term duration of up to one month and 50 weekly installments or 12 EMI in case of loans of 1-year tenure.
1. What is the maximum amount of loan available?
The maximum amount of loan available is twice the amount of deposit the borrower has with the bank.
2. Who can avail this loan?
The borrower must have a deposit of Rs 500 to avail this loan.
