Eligibility:

Any Resident Individual - Single Accounts, Two or more individuals in Joint Accounts, Illiterate Persons, Visually Impaired persons, Purdanasheen Ladies, Minors, Associations, Clubs, Societies, etc. Trusts, Institutions/Agencies specifically permitted by the RBI.

SALIENT FEATURES :

  • Simplest deposit option available to the depositor.
  • ATM cum Debit card issued without any charge.
  • 2 Free Cheque Books per year
  • Free Accidental Insurance cover by Debit Card usage.
  • Tabulous Banking facility to open accounts from your home/office *
  • New Generation banking facilities
    • Any Where / Any Time Banking through -Branch Banking/ATM Banking/Net Banking/Phone Banking.
    • Free Internet Banking Facility – Unique facility of Self User Creation & Password Reset.
    • Self generation of ATM pin for customers by calling the toll free IVR using their registered mobile number.
    • Remittance products on ATMs viz. NEFT, IMPS, Union e-Cash and Investments into Mutual funds.
    • U- Mobile facility – facilitates Balance Enquiry, NEFT, Bill Payment, Mobile Recharge etc. through your mobile phone.
    • Earn Rewardz points on your Debit card spend.
  • Zero balance facility. A Student can open Regular Saving Account with zero balance.
  • Provision for nomination.

Forms & Documents

  • Q1. How do I approach to open a Savings Account?
    Ans. You can approach us in any of the following ways:
    • Apply online
    • Call us at our 24x7 Call Centre, on phone numbers given at the top of the screen
    • You can just walk into any of our branches.
  • Q2. At what frequency will the interest be paid to me?
    Ans. Interest @ 4% p.a, will be calculated on daily product basis from 01.04.2010 and will be credited twice in a year in Feb and August or at the time of closing A/c.
  • Q3. Whom can I include as Joint Applicants?
    Ans. Anyone can be included as a joint applicant for a Savings Account except a minor (less than 18 years of age).
  • Q4. What are the nomination facilities available on a Savings Account?
    • Nomination facility available for bank deposits.
    • There can be only one Nominee for a deposit account whether held singly or jointly.
    • A person legally empowered to operate a minor's account can file a nomination on behalf of the minor.
    • Applicants can make nomination by filling up the Form prescribed under the Banking Companies (Nomination) Rules 1985.
    • The nomination details can be changed during the subsistence of the account relationship by filling up the Form prescribed under the Banking Companies (Nomination) Rules 1985.
    • For more details approach your Bank branch.
  • Q5. How can I change the nominee(s) for my Savings Account?
    Ans. You can change the nominee(s) by making a declaration to that effect, in the appropriate form, which is available at any of the Union Bank branches where you are having your Savings account.
  • Q6. Do I have any facility for Standing Instructions?
    Ans. Yes, you can give standing instructions through your Savings Account. A few examples of the same are as below:
    • Transfer to a Fixed Deposit.
    • Transfer to a Recurring Deposit
    • Make recurring payments like LIC premium, Installments etc.
  • Q7. What are the different channels through which I can access my Savings Account?
    You can access your Savings Account through:
    • Branch Banking
    • ATM Banking
    • Net Banking
    • Phone Banking
  • Q8. How to update the new address in my Saving Accounts?
    Ans. Any one of the following documents can be submitted for address change:
    • Valid Passport
    • Voter ID
    • Driving License
    • Electricity bill
    • Telephone bill
    • Ration card
    • Any other valid proof as acceptable to the Bank
