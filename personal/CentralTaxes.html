Physical payment at branches

We are authorized to accept Collection under Direct Taxes like Income Tax , Corporate Tax, Interest Tax, Expenditure Tax, Estate Duty Tax and Gift Tax etc. Our “1062 branches” Pan India are designated to accept Direct Tax Collection from the assesses. The list of the designated branches as under

click for authorised branches

Assesses while remitting amounts should use the prescribed Challan from, duly completed in all respects. The remittances can be made either by Cash, Cheque or Drafts.

Following Points are to be taken care while filling up the challan.
  • Major head of account should be correctly mentioned
  • Name, Address, Assessment, Year, ITO Ward/ Circle, PAN / GIR Number, Amount in words and figures should be clearly furnished
  • Tax Payer must use the new single copy challan from (280, 281 & 282)
    1. Challan No.280 for Corporation Tax & Income Tax.
    2. Challan No.281 for Tax Deduction at Source (TDS) under major heads 0020 & 0021.
    3. Challan No.282 for Wealth Tax/ gift tax/expenditure tax etc.

* Tax Payer can counter check from NSDL website whether taxes paid by him / her have been reported to TIN correctly at www.tin_nsdl.com by using his/her Challan Identification Number (CIN).
