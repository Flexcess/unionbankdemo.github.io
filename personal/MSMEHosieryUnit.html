HOSEIRY UNITS IN KOLKATA

PURPOSE:-

This is a special scheme for Hoseiry units in Kolkata. Under this scheme you get the facility to replace or purchase a new machinery/equipment and / or you can avail this loan to meet Working Capital needs of the units.

ELIGIBILITY:-
  • Proposed activity should be situated in and around Kolkata
  • Present activity should be profitable for the last 3 years
  • Proposed activity should be technically feasible and economically viable
QUANTUM OF LOAN:-

  • Q. Who can avail loan under the Scheme and for what purpose?

    A. Present and Proposed Hosiery units situated in and around Kolkata are covered under the scheme. The unit should be running profitably for the last 3 years with increase in sales turnover and the proposed activity should be technically feasible and economically viable having minimum Credit Rating of CR-3 and above. The scheme is meant for replacement or purchase of new machinery/equipment and to meet Working Capital needs of the units.

  • Q. What is the quantum of finance?

    A. Maximum amount that can be financed by way of Term Loan and Working Capital is Rs. 20 lac within which Working Capital limits should not exceed Rs. 5 lac.

  • Q. Is there any margin requirement for availing finance under the scheme?

    A. Yes. The minimum margin requirement is 15% for both Term Loan and Working Capital.

  • Q. What will be the repayment period?

    A. The term loan will be repaid within a maximum period of 84 months with a moratorium period of six months. The working capital will be renewed / reviewed every year.
