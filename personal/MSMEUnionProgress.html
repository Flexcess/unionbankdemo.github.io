1.  Scope
  • The scheme will be operational Pan India.
  • All types of Micro Enterprises, e.g. units engaged in manufacturing , processing , preservation of goods, retail trade, professional self employed persons, SRTOs, construction/supply contractors, educational institutions, etc
2.  Eligibility
  • Micro Units engaged in Manufacturing and Service Sector. For Manufacturing Sector, Original investment in core Plant & Machinery excluding cost of items as specified in MSE policy for calculating investment in Plant & Machinery should be upto Rs.25 Lac and for Service Sector, Original investment in equipment upto Rs.10 Lac.
  • Proprietorship, Partnership concerns (including Limited Liability Partnerships), Limited Companies, etc. belonging to MICRO Category.
  • Units should have all the statutory approvals/NOCs from the respective Departments etc.
  • Credit Rating of the borrower should not be below CR- 4 for takeover and CR- 5 in case of new connections.
  • Any deviation in take over norms shall be subject to norms prescribed in latest Loan Policy.
  • All new cases of Micro Enterprise credit proposals shall be covered under the scheme.
3.  Purpose To meet business related needs including purchase/construction of business premises, machinery, equipments, vehicles and working capital requirements of all types of Micro Enterprises.
4.  Nature of Facilities Term Loan and/or Working Capital
5.  Quantum Of Finance Suitable limits can be sanctioned based on the requirements and financials of the borrower to be assessed as per the lending methods indicated in the Loan Policy.
6.  Margin
  • 5%, for credit limit upto Rs.10 lacs.
  • 15% = for credit limits above Rs.10 lacs
7.  Interest Rate As per extant Interest Rate circular.
     
8.  Processing Charges Amount of advance Processing Charges
Upto Rs.10 lacs NIL
Above Rs.10 lacs Flat Rs. 1000/-
9.  Documentation & Inspection Charges   Flat Rs. 500 + actual stamp duty if any.
10.  Credit Rating Norms In respect of advances having total credit limits above Rs.2.00 lacs, Credit Rating shall be done as per extant guidelines.
11.  Take Over Norms As per Bankâ€™s loan policy
12.  Security Primary:
  • All assets created out of banks finance shall be charged in favor of bank by way of hypothecation /mortgage etc.
  • Collateral â€“ All eligible cases for CGTMSE to be mandatorily covered under CGTMSE and annual Guarantee Fee payable to Credit Guarantee Trust to be fully absorbed by the Bank (as against present 0.50% borne by the borrower).
  • Where the CGTMSE cover is not available such as retail trade, educational institutions, SHG/JLGs, collateral security to the extent of min.60% of total credit facility by way of securities like term deposit, NSC, assignment of life policies, mortgage of properties shall be obtained.
  • Personal guarantee of Promoter Directors, Proprietor, Partners of the firm/company having sufficient means and of all mortgagors of collateral security.
  • If CGTMSE cover is available, no third party guarantee shall be taken.
13.  Appraisal The assessment of credit limits shall be done as per methods stipulated in the Loan Policy.
14.  Repayment Working Capital / Term Loan limits:
- 12 months for WC and subject to renewal as per extant guidelines.
- Term Loans to be repaid within a maximum period of 84 monthly installments inclusive of moratorium period.
15.  Delegation As per the existing scheme of delegation of loaning powers.
16.  Due Diligence Due diligence to be carried out as per the extant guidelines of bank.
17.  Reporting All sanctions shall be reported to the controlling office in M-27 on monthly basis.
