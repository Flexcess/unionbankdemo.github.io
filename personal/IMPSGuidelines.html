FAQS FOR IMPS

USER GUIDELINES FOR IMPS
  • What is IMPS?

    Immediate Payment Service (IMPS) is an instant interbank electronic fund transfer service available through mobile phones/ATM/ branch/ internet banking

  • How to avail IMPS?

    To avail the facility of IMPS, please register your mobile number with the Bank for mobile banking

Transfer of Funds using IMPS:
You can transfer fund to other bank account through IMPS using the following options
  1. P2A ( Person to Account number)

    Using this option , you can send money to any bank account by just entering account number and IFSC code of the beneficiary

  2. P2P ( Person to person)

    Using this option, you can send money to any bank account using mobile number and MMID of the beneficiary.

PARTICIPATING BANKS OF IMPS

The list of banks offering IMPS is available in the website of National Payment Corporation of India (NPCI) www.npci.org.in.. under the section -http://www.npci.org.in/bankmember.aspx
