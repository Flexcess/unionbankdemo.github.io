Digi Purse

  • Features
  • FAQ
  • Terms & Conditions

Use your Union Bank DigiPurse wallet for bill payments, recharges, shopping or even to send money. Simply add money from your debit card, credit card, internet banking and IMPS into your DigiPurse wallet to get started. One app for all utility payments, DTH, mobile recharge, post-paid bills, gas bills etc., Payments from one wallet to another, one wallet to other bank account and instant money transfer through IMPS. DigiPurse wallet has a virtual Visa card which enables users to transact on any website for ecommerce transaction.

  • Currently, there are no charges for using Union Bank DigiPurse wallet
  • Initial top up amount is Rs.500/-
  • Maximum top up amount is Rs. 10,000/-
  • Daily fund transfer limit – Rs.5,000/-

Availability

DigiPurse App is available on android and ios and shall soon be available on Windows

  1. How do I download the app?

    2. Android: Visit Google Play-store from your Android device and search for DigiPurse by Union Bank. Click on install and accept all permissions.

    IOS: Visit App Store from your Apple device and search for DigiPurse by Union Bank and click on install.

  2. Do I need to have internet activated on my phone, even for using the app after downloading it onto my phone

    3. Yes, internet services will be used for accessing DigiPurse.

  3. Is there any charge for registering on DigiPurse?

    4. No, DigiPurse is a free app, there is no charge for registration and usage of the app.

  4.  I didn’t receive any OTP, how do I create my account?

    5. We have an option called “Resend OTP” on the same page in the mobile app where you would need to input the OTP. Kindly tap on “Resend” for re-initiating an OTP to your mobile number.

  5.  What are the features on the DigiPurse?

    6. The following services are available on DigiPurse

    1. Prepaid mobile recharge & DTH
    2. Bill Payment
    3. Fund transfer
    4. View your statements
    5. View Card details
    6. Add Money
    7. VISA Virtual prepaid card for online shopping.

  6. Where can I view my Virtual Prepaid card?

    7. Your card is available in the menu option “ View Card “ of DigiPurse wallet.

  7. Where can I use my virtual prepaid card?

    8. Your virtual prepaid card can be used for online shopping  across websites in India.

  8.  Is there any amount limit when I am sending money?

    9. Yes, there is a limit for fund transfer which is restricted to a maximum amount of Rs. 5,000 per day.
