PURPOSE

Avail Union Smile to meet short term financial requirements such as unforeseen medical expenses, timely payment of electricity bill, telephone bill, school fees, water charges, etc. by drawing overdraft on your SB account with your bank.

ELIGIBILITY

A person has to meet the following criteria to avail the loan:
  • Indian citizen above 21 years.
  • Salaried people who have salary account with our bank.
  • Or Pensioners who draw their pension from our bank.

QUANTUM OF LOAN

Maximum of 90% of one month's salary / pension

MARGIN

10%

MORATORIUM PERIOD

NIL

REPAYMENT

  • Entire overdraft amount plus interest to be recovered from next month's salary / pension.
  • If requested, repayment by EMIs with a maximum repayment period of 3 months can be permitted

RATE OF INTEREST AND PROCESSING CHARGES

  •   To know rate of interest, please click here

  • There are no processing charges.

SECURITY

No Secuirty required

GUARANTEE

No guarantee is required

INSURANCE

No insurance is required.

**Conditions apply. For further details, please visit our nearest branch.

What is the maximum loan amount?
The maximum amount of loan sanctioned would be 90% of one month's salary or pension credited to the account.
What are the loan tenure options?
The overdraft amount plus interest would be recovered from the next month's salary or pension. If the borrower opts for payment through EMIs, then repayment tenure of 3 months is allowed.
What are the applicable rates of interest?
Please click here for information on rate of interest.
What are the processing charges?
There are no processing charges on this loan.
How is the interest charged/calculated?
Interest will be applied in the account on the basis of reducing balance method at monthly rests.
What security/ collateral do I have to provide?
No security is required.
Is a guarantor a must?
No, guarantee is not required.
What documents do I need to submit to avail a loan?
Please see the Forms & Documents Tab above for information on the list of documents required.
How much time will it take for my loan to be approved?
It takes 5 days for your loan to be sanctioned, after you have submitted all the documents.
What are the stages involved in taking a loan?
There are three main stages -
  • Application - Whereby you submit a completely filled in application with all necessary documents.
  • Sanction - Whereby you get an approval for a specific loan amount based on the value of your property and repayment capabilities.
  • Disbursement - Whereby the loan amount is disbursed.
