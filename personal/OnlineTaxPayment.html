  • 3. How to get the challan if I failed to print at the time of payment?

    Please log on to your e banking account and click on tax payment menu to generate the challan.

    Only challans relating to income tax/advance tax, service tax and central excise can be generated.

    In case of failed transaction payment usually gets back to the account within 3 to 4 working days.

    Please call our 24x7 help line for any queries. You may also mail the transaction details to etax@unionbankofindia.com for resolution of issues.
