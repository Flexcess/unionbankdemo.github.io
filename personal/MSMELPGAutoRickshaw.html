LPG Autorickshaw

  • Features
  • FAQ
PURPOSE:-

Under this scheme loan is granted for purchasing LPG driven, 3 seated, new auto rickshaws under loan cum subsidy scheme. Applicant should be a resident of Chennai metropolitan area.

ELIGIBILITY:-
  • Applicant should be a resident of Chennai metropolitan area
  • Scheme is applicable for driver cum owner only
  • Age of applicant should be between 25 and 40
  • Applicant should possess valid driving license
  • Applicant should not already possess any auto rickshaw permit
  • Family income of applicant should not exceed Rs. 36000/- p.a.
More than one permit shall not be allotted to the same family under this scheme
* Preference will be given to Women and SC/ST candidates
QUANTUM OF LOAN:-

Loan amount is restricted up to 90% of the cost of vehicle subject to maximum of Rs. 1,08,000/-

* Cost of vehicle inclusive of Insurance, Registration, Road Tax and cost of digital and fare meter
MARGIN, I.E. YOUR SHARE:-
10% of cost of the vehicle.
NATURE OF FACILITY:-
Working Capital / Term Loan.

SECURITY:-

  • Hypothecation of vehicle. Bank lien will be marked on RC book of the vehicle and also in the permit issued by Regional transport authority
  • The Subsidy (back ended) of Rs. 25000/-will be treated as collateral security

REPAYMENT:-
60 equal monthly installments with maximum moratorium period of 3 months

  • Q. For what purpose the loan is granted?

    A. The loan is granted for purchasing LPG driven, 3 seated, new auto rickshaw under loan cum subsidy scheme.

  • Q. Who can avail loan under the Scheme?

    A. Loan is available to the resident of Chennai metropolitan area aged between 25-40 years having valid driving license with badge number. The applicant should not already possess any auto rickshaw permit and his/her family income should not exceed Rs. 36000/- p.a. The scheme is applicable for driver cum owner only.

  • Q. What is the quantum of finance?

    A. Loan amount is restricted up to 90% of the cost of vehicle subject to maximum of Rs. 1,08,000/-.

  • Q. Is there any stipulation of collaterals?

    A. No Collateral required under the scheme. However, the back ended subsidy of Rs. 25000/-will be treated as collateral security.

  • Q. What will be the repayment period?

    A. The loan will be repaid in 60 equated monthly installment.
