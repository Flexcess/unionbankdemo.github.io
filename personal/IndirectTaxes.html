Direct Tax Collection

We are authorized to accept Collection under Direct Taxes like Income Tax , Corporate Tax, Interest Tax, Expenditure Tax, Estate Duty Tax and Gift Tax etc.609 branches throughout country are designated to accept Direct Tax Collection from the assesses. The list of the designated branches are as under.

Assesses while remitting amounts should use the prescribed Challan from, duly completed in all respects. The remittances can be made either by Cash, Cheque or Drafts.

Following Points are to be taken care while filling up the challan.

  1. Major head of account should be correctly mentioned.
  2. Name, Address, Assessment, Year, ITO Ward/ Circle, PAN / GIR Number, Amount in words and figures should be clearly furnished.
  3. Tax Payer must use the new single copy challan from (280, 281 & 282)
  4. Tax Payer can counter check from NSDL website whether taxes paid by him / her have been reported to TIN correctly at www.tin_nsdl.com by using his/her Challan Identification Number (CIN).
