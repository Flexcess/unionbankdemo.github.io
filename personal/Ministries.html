FUND MANAGEMENT SOLUTION MINISTRIES

Accredited Ministries
As accredited banker, Bank is committed to extend all kinds of banking
services to their various offices/ Department affiliated
bodies across the country. Four ministries are:
  1. Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change

  2. Ministry of Labour &Employment

  3. Ministry of Statistics & Program Implementation:

  4. Ministry of Science & Technology
    • Department of Science & Technology (DST)
    • Department of Bio – Technology (DBT)
    • Department of Science & Industrial Research (DSIR)
Other Ministries
Other than accredited ministries, there are few more ministries on whose behalf Union Bank is collecting online fees. These ministries are as under:
  1. Ministry of Corporate Affairs:

    • Collection of fees & registration charges on behalf of Ministry of Corporate Affairs.

  2. Ministry of Home Affairs :

    • Authorized for collection of Visa charges.

  3. Ministry of External Affairs:

    • Collection of charges from high commission or representatives on behalf of Ministry of External Affairs.

  4. Railway :

    • Authorized for collection of Railway Bulk Freight Payment.
