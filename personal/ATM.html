As a Union Bank ATM card holder, you can perform banking transactions round the clock. Additionally, Union Bank ATMs accept all VISA / Master / Rupay / EMV Debit/Credit Cards.

Union Bank ATMs allowsUnion Bank Debit Card holders to perform the following
  • Cash Withdrawal
  • Balance Enquiry
  • Mini Statement
  • Change of PIN
  • Transfer of funds to other Union Bank accounts linked to the card
  • Registration for mobile banking
  • Payment of utility bills
  • Request for Cheque books and Account Statements
  • Inter-Bank Card to Card fund transfer
  • NEFT and IMPS
  • Mutual Fund Payments
  • Aadhaar Seeding
  • Pay taxes, and many more
Other bank card holders can also withdraw cash up to Rs. 10,000 per day and check their account balance at Union Bank ATMs

CONTACT NUMBERS

If you have any queries or complaints, feel free to contact us on our 24 x 7 Call Centre , the numbers are given at the top of the screen

  1. Is there a Union Bank ATM at my location?

    Union Bank has installed more than 6900 ATMs across the country and it is quite likely that you will find a Union Bank ATM near your location. Please click here to go to ATM Search

  2. Is there any limit on withdrawals or are there any charges for cash withdrawals for other bank card holders?

    RBI allows other bank card holders to withdraw up to Rs. 10000 per day and five free withdrawals at other bank ATMs per month are permitted by RBI for saving bank card holders

  3. Sometimes money is not dispensed at ATMs but my account gets debited?

    In very rare cases, due to network failures this problem might occur. It is also possible that amount is debited twice for a given transaction. If you are a Union Bank Card holder, all such complaints with the details of Card Number, Account Number, Date of transaction, Amount of transaction can be raised at our 24 Call Centre [phone numbers at the top of the screen] or nearest branch and the problem shall be resolved with in 12 working days from the date of complaint. The format for furnishing details of such failed transactions is available at ATMs

    However if you have performed transactions using some other card, please contact your card issuing branch and raise a complaint.

  4. What happens if I enter a wrong PIN or forget my ATM PIN?

    When wrong PIN is used 3 times on any given day, the card gets blocked for that particular day. The user can try the card on the next day. If the roblem exists, please equest for a duplicate PIN at our 24 hour helpline number and the same will be sent to a branch nearest to you.

  5. What are the possible reasons for my card not working at ATMs?

    The card might not be working if :

    • The balance is insufficient
    • The daily limit is reached
    • Network failures
    • Wrong PIN
    • Damaged magnetic strip
    • Card is hot listed

    If there is any damage to the card, you can request for duplicate card at any branch or at the Call Center.
