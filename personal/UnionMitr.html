UNION MITR

  • FAQ

Objective

Union Mitr is especially designed for the rural popation. It facilitates financial education to all strata of people.

Services offered

  • Information on financial products, services and where they are available.
  • Guidance on opening a bank A/C.
  • Information on products of bank including interest rates and charges.
  • Information on managing savings.
  • Guidance about management of existing debt.
  • Information on likely avenues of investment.

Utility

Financial education brings about improvements in spending habits, saving habits and investment habits in individuals. As a whole, it improves the life of the individual.

1. How and when one will get counseling?

The beneficiary may call at Village Knowledge Center during office hours & spell out problems in detail, without any reservation. If need be, necessary supporting papers may also be submitted. The individual is also free to contact the centre on telephone.

2. How much will this service cost?

Service is absolutely free. Customer Satisfaction is Union Bank of India's motto.

3. Will only existing bank customers get help?

Any person, whether he is the Bank's customer or not, can avail of this service.
