UNION PRAGATI - Loan through FINO :

Objective

Union Pragati solely intends the progress of women. It is a Microloan Product for joint liability group comprising of 5 married women. It is designed to meet the financial requirements of women Smart Card holders covered under financial inclusion project, towards meeting their business, consumption needs. It is also an initiative to free them from the clutches of moneylenders.

Eligibility

  • Women of low income group.
  • She should be a Smart Card holder.
  • Age between 21 to 59 years.
  • Has regular income not seasonal & are in the business/ occupation for at least 1 year.

Quantum of loan

Minimum loan amount of Rs. 12,000 and maximum loan amount is Rs. 25,000. Loan amounts can be considered as per the requirement, and repayment capacity of the beneficiary.

Margin

Nil

Security

  • DP Note (SD-21C)
  • Mutual Guarantee from the group members

Rate Of Interest

At present (Base rate +0.25 %) on reducing Balance

Processing charges- 1% of loan amount to FINO.

Repayment

For both the amounts, repayment period is 26-52 fortnightly installments

List Of Documents

  • Application form

  • DP Note

  • Reference

1. Is it an individual loan or a group loan?
This is a group loan.
 
2. Is there any insurance facility available with this scheme?
An insurance coverage of Rs. 20,000 to borrower & her spouse will be given along with this scheme. The borrower has to pay Rs. 160 per year for this insurance coverage.
