LOAN AGAINST WAREHOUSE RECEIPT

  • Features
  • Forms & Documents
  • FAQ

Loan against receipt of warehouse/cold storage is available to provide liquidity to the farmers and prevent them from resorting to distress sale of their produce at the time of harvest.

ELIGIBILITY

  • Existing/Prospective customers of the Bank viz. proprietorship concerns/partnership firms/corporate /individuals trading in agriculture commodities.
  • Arthias in rural and semi urban areas
  • Food and Agro based processing units using Agricultural Commodities as raw materials

QUANTUM OF LOAN

Up to 50%-75% of the value of commodities. Value of commodities shall be the lower of the following:

Acreage (Irrigated Lands) Maximum loan limit*
1. Value of commodities based on the prices from the nearest Govt. approved mandi (APMC) on the date of disbursement.
OR
2. Price cap of a commodity communicated by Bank.

RATE OF INTEREST

Please contact the nearest Union Bank branch for details

Repayment

Each drawl has to be repaid within 10 months. Though repayment period of 10 months is allowed, it is advisable to get the drawls adjusted before arrival of new crop.

Security

Pledge of Warehouse Receipts of approved warehouses. No other collateral is required.

Documents like DPN, Pledge agreement, Declaration by the borrowers and Letter of continuity once obtained is valid for 3 years. Warehouse Receipt endorsed in favour of Bank, Lien marked in the books of warehouse, Take delivery letter is required for each warehouse receipt against which finance will be made. Stamping fee will be according to norm of the state.
