Union Super Salary Account (USSA) - One Account, Multiple Benefits

  • Zero Balance Account, No minimum balance requirement.
  • Personalised Multicity Cheque Books.
  • Internet Banking Facility - Unique facility of Self User Creation & Password Reset
  • Comprehensive Welcome Kit is provided to the USSA holder at the time of opening. The welcome kit contains: Debit Card, User manuals for Internet Banking, Phone Banking, SMS Banking, and Mobile banking.
  • Debit Card - Issued free of charge.
  • Free Accidental Insurance cover by Debit Card usage.
  • Any Where / Any Time Banking through -Branch Banking/ATM Banking/Net Banking/Phone Banking.
  • Overdraft facility to the extent of 90% of the net salary credited in the previous month, subject to a ceiling of Rs 25,000/-, which will be considered at the discretion of the Bank.
  • USSA opens doors for a wide range of additional financial services, such as Retail Loans (Home, Vehicle and Education), Insurance (Life/Non-life), Mutual Funds, Demat, Online Trading etc. subject to usual terms.
  • Tabulous Banking Facility to open their account from Office Premises. (This facility is available at select centres)
