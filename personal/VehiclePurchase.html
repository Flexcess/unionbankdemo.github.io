SCHEME FOR FINANCING PURCHASE OF TWO, THREE & FOUR WHEELERS

We provide loans to agriculturists in order to purchase two/three/four wheelers/trucks/jeeps/pick-up vans etc.

ELIGIBILITY

  • The applicant should be an agriculturist having sizeable land.
  • Agriculturists having scattered land holding/land in two or more villages in more than one piece/farm situated far away from residence.
  • Agriculturists following intensive and multiple cropping systems.
  • Agriculturists adopting heterogeneous activities, mixed farming practices such as dairy, poultry, fisheries, sericulture, bee keeping, vegetable farm, horticulture, plantation or other such activities with seasonal crops.
  • Agriculturists staying far away from agriculture produce markets/other markets.
  • The applicant should be from service area village/within operational area of branch.

Quantum of loan

75% OF THE COST OF THE VEHICLE.

Margin

25% of the cost of the vehicle, excluding insurance charges and registration charges, etc.

RATE OF INTEREST AND PROCESSING CHARGES

  •   To know rate of interest, please click here

Repayment

3-5 years on half yearly/quarterly/monthly installments coinciding with the surplus available with the farmer due to harvesting/marketing of agriculture produce or otherwise.

LIST OF DOCUMENTS

  • Up to Rs. 1,00,000/- DPN, Hypothecation of assets created and crops.
  • Over Rs. 1,00,000/- DPN, Hypothecation of assets created and crops, mortgage of land &/or third party guarantee.
  • Equitable mortgage of Cold Storage/Block Assets.
  • Personal Guarantee of applicant/ Directors/Partners.

1. What is the maximum loan amount?
75% of the cost of the vehicle.
2. How will the installments be decided?
The installments will be decided based on the surplus available with the farmer based on harvest or marketing of agriculture output.
