REWARD PROGRAM FOR DEBIT/CREDIT CARD

Get Started with Union Rewardz

Banking with Union Bank has always been a pleasurable experience for our valued patrons. Time has come whenthis experience amplifies even further and elevates things to a whole new dimension with optimum value addition. And all this begins with Union Rewardz – The very own loyalty program initiated by Union Bank of India for our Debit and Credit Card holders. Now enjoy the privilege of accruing reward points on all transactions made through your Debit & Credit Cards at POS and E-Commerce outlets. These points then can be redeemed against thousands of merchandise items, movie, bus & air tickets, gift vouchers & more.

A Time to Rejoice!

The best part of Union Rewardz is that as a Debit or Credit Card holder of Union Bank you are automatically pre-enrolled as its member.This entitles you to reap amazing benefits whenever you choose to! Isn’t that a reason enough to celebrate with gusto! All this and more begins with your registration at www.UnionRewardz.com or by calling 18001088000.

Get into the Reward Cycle

Whenever you go shopping or for that instance get any reason to spend, do so with your Union Bank Debit or Credit Card. Why? The reasons are plenty! Firstly you can enjoy the liberty of having a hassle free and cashless transaction with your savings account earning interest till the date of purchase. With every purchase perRs. 100, you tend to earn Union Points. These Union Points can then be exchanged for a plethora of products and services and other bountiful rewards.

How to Accrue your UnionPoints

As a member of Union Rewardz you automatically become eligible to accrue points whenever you make spends at Point of Sales (POS) or during online purchases using Union Bank of India’s Debit or Credit Card.You earn these Union Points when at least a minimum of Rs. 100 is spent.

How to Redeem your Union Points

To start with the process of redemption, you will first have to accrue a minimum of 100 Union Points to make the first transaction. From there on it becomes a continuous cycle. The Union Points can be used to redeem a vast array of merchandize, movie, bus & air tickets, gift vouchers & much more.

How much Union Points do you earn on your Union Bank Credit Card?

The point slab has been structured in a manner so that you can now earn Union Points in proportion to your basic monthly expenditure, done using your Union Bank Credit Card. For e.g. if your monthly spend through your Credit Card is up to Rs. 10,000, then you accrue 1 Union Point for every Rs. 100 spent. Subsequently, as soon as your spend falls within the slab of Rs. 10,001 to Rs. 30,000, your accrual of Union Points increase to 1.5 for every Rs. 100 spent. If your basic monthly spend surpasses Rs. 30,000 the scale further tilts towards a higher side with you acquiring 2 Union Points on every Rs. 100 spent.

The Reward Point Structure is subject to change from time to time at the discretion of the Bank.
How much Union Points do you earn on your Union Bank Debit Card?
Starting 1st April’13, Union Bank debit card reward programme is revised as under:
  • Debit card holders will earn one reward point for every Rs.100/- spent at PoS/Internet.
  • 100 Bonus points to the first time users of debit cards at PoS/Internet provided they spend at PoS/Internet atleast 3 times during the same month.

The Reward Point Structure is subject to change from time to time at the discretion of the Bank.
