ECGC Credit Insurance Product - ECGC Corporate Agency

Union Bank has tied up with Export Credit Guarantee Corporation Ltd (ECGC) for marketing of credit insurance products. ECGC is offering various credit insurance products to Indian exporters to protect them from risk of loss involved in export trade

This is another innovative initiative with an aim to provide value added service to cater the needs of our exporters. Now all our export customers can have the following popular policies at more convenient delivery points and hassle free environment.

  1. Standard Policy.
  2. Small Exporter's policy.
  3. Specific shipment policies - Short Term.
  4. Exports (Specific Buyer) Policy.
  5. Exports Turnover Policy.
