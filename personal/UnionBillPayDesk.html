UNION BILL PAY (THROUGH BILL DESK)

  • Features
  • FAQ

Union Bill Pay is the easiest way to make your bill payments. It enables you to make bill payments at your doorstep.

Union Bill Pay through Billdesk.com
Features & Benefits
  • Instant bill payments
  • Available 24x7

Eligibility

This facility is available to all Internet banking users with transaction facility.

Service Charge

Union Bank provides this facility free of charge.
However certain companies may charge depending on their rules and procedures
e.g. IRCTC ticket charges Rs.10/- per ticket
  • 1. What is bill payment?

    Ans: It includes utility bill payments like electricity bills, telephone bills, insurance premium payments, online Railway/Air ticket bookings etc.

    Please note that this facility is available to all Internet banking users with transaction facility.

  • 2. Which bills can be paid with Union Bill Pay through Billdesk ?

    Ans: Please register with Billdesk at http://www.billdesk.com/unionbank/ubi.htm and check the biller list provided

  • 3. In case of failed bill payment what should I do?

    Ans: Please call our 24x7 call center and register your complaint by giving the necessary details.

  • 4. How many days it takes to revert back the failed transaction amount?

    Ans: The refund of failed transaction amount depends on the biller, however usually it takes 7 to 14 days.

  • 5. Whether any registration is required for using this service.

    Ans: Yes, you must register with Billdesk, for which no payment is required.

  • 6. Whom should I approach to clarify my queries?

    Ans: You may directly view the Billdesk website, or contact our 24x7 call center to know the procedure and other details.
