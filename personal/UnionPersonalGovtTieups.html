PERSONAL LOAN UNDER TIE-UPS WITH CENTRAL / STATE GOVERNMENT & REPUTED ORGANIZATIONS

PURPOSE

There are many among us who might be facing difficulty in purchasing goods by paying a lumpsum amount. But we would be comfortable paying small installments on a monthly basis. The Union Personal loan plays an important role here. It helps you avail loans to meet personal expenses such as purchase of consumer durable, etc.

ELIGIBILITY

To avail "Union Personal under Tie-up with Central / State Government & Reputed Organizations" you need to meet the following criteria:
  • Should be Permanent/Confirmed employee of Central/ State Government/ autonomous bodies/ Public/ Joint Sector Undertakings/ Reputed domestic/ Indian subsidiaries of foreign companies/ Educational institutions etc. having regular source of income.
  • The applicant has reasonable residual service to ensure that the entire loan is repaid one year prior to retirement.

QUANTUM OF LOAN

Up to Rs. 10 Lakh subject to the repayment capacity.

MARGIN, I.E. YOUR SHARE

Nil

MORATORIUM PERIOD

Nil

REPAYMENT

Repayment period of up to 5 years

RATE OF INTEREST AND PROCESSING CHARGES

  •   To know rate of interest, please click here

  • Processing charges are 0.50% of the loan amount, subject to a minimum of Rs. 500, plus service tax.

PREPAYMENT PENALTY

  • There is NO prepayment penalty if the loan is adjusted by the borrower from his own verifiable legitimate sources

  • If the loan is taken over by any other Bank /FI, then 1.00% higher rate of interest to be charged ab-initio till date of take over.

GUARANTEE

2 Guarantees are required.*

SECURITY

No security other than the guarantees and undertaking from employee & employer.
