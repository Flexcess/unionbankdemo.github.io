DEFENCE PENSION

  • Pension Payment Orders (PPO) issued by PCDA Allahabad, CDA Navy Mumbai and CDA Air force for Army, Navy and Air force respectively.
  • The PPOs are sent to CPPC Mumbai for pension payment to the Pension Account number which the pensioner has already furnished.
  • Pension is periodically paid through Central Pension Processing Cell, Mumbai.
  • For any clarifications/grievances pensioners may approach his/her pension paying branches.
