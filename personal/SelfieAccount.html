Union Selfie & m Passbook

Union Bank of India has launched “Union Selfie” app which enables you to open your Union Bank Savings Accounts through this app on your smartphone.

What you need to do is to just to download the Union Selfie & M passbook app from the playstore or appstore, install and open it, enter your mobile number and accept the terms & conditions by clicking on the box.

The app prompts you to select your nearest branch, Income Range and your e-mail address and submit. You will receive a One Time Password (OTP) on your mobile for verification. Now, take your “Selfie”, take a snap of the front side of your Aadhaar card and the quick response code (Black Rectangular box) available on the Aadhaar card. Now, take the snapshot of the address portion of Aadhaar card and the front side of your PAN Card and submit.

Congratulations! Your savings account gets opened and the account number is generated.

Now, visit your chosen branch with the original Aadhaar card and PAN card for activation of your account.
