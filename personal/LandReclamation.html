Loan for Land Reclamation and Development provides hassle-free finance for land leveling, reclamation, bunding, and other soil conservation developmental activity.

ELIGIBILITY

Farmers having own land or cultivating Registered leased land.

QUANTUM OF LOAN

As per NABARD, the quantum of loan depends on the unit cost with applicable margins.

Margin

Up to Rs.25,000 -- Nil
Above Rs. 25,000/- and upto Rs. 50,000/- -- 5 to 10 %
Above Rs. 50,000/- -- 10% to 15%.

Security Documents

DPN, Mortgage of Land and/or third party guarantee.

Repayment

3 to 5 years (Moratorium is permitted, to coincide with harvesting of crops/marketing of produce).

1. Are all farmers eligible for the loan?
Farmers having their own land and farmers having farms in registered leased lands are only eligible for this loan.
2. Is there any moratorium period available for the loan?
Moratorium will be decided considering the crop harvest or crop marketing period.
