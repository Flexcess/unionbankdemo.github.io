NEFT THROUGH ATM

With our NEFT facility at our ATM, now you can transfer funds without the need to visit the branch. NEFT facility can be availed at any of our ATMs during following timings:

 Timings:   8.00 Hrs to 18.00 Hrs (Monday to Friday).
8.00 Hrs to 12.30 Hrs (Saturday).
On Sundays and RBI holidays NEFT will not be available.
Subject to change of timing from time to time.

Fund Transfer Limit:

Minimum Amount Rs.1/- Maximum amount   Rs.50000/-

Easy steps for NEFT through ATM – Registration

  • Registration :
    For Registration   click the link:
    https://securevas.fssnet.co.in/APS/ubicustomerlogin.htm

  • Select option-
      New User-Register Here
      • Fill in the required data in the page. Particulars  mark with (*) are mandatory .
      • Click on Submit button and enter particulars of beneficiary details in next page.
      • Click on Next Button and after successful registration e mail id & password will be send to your registered email Id.
      • After Successful Registration for customer and beneficiary customer you can do   NEFT transactions using any of the Union bank ATMs.
 
Steps for NEFT transaction though ATM
    • Visit any of our ATMs
    • Select  “OTHER” Option.
    • Select Option Fund Transfer
    • Select Option NEFT   PAYMENT
    • Select Beneficiary no.
    • Confirm Beneficiary
    • Enter Amount to be transferred.
    • Confirm Amount of transfer
    • Select Account no. From Option Savings OR Checking
    • Fund is transferred and receipt is generated.
