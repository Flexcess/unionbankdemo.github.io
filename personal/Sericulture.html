LOAN FOR SERICULTURE

Loan for Sericulture helps provide hassle-free and adequate credit for Mulberry cultivation, purchase of equipment, construction of rearing house, establishment of reeling unit, purchase of eggs, etc.

ELIGIBILITY

Farmers cultivating own land/registered leased land with irrigation facilities for cultivation of mulberry.

Quantum of loan

As per NABARD, the quantum of loan will include unit cost approved.

Margin

Up to Rs. 1,00,000/- - Nil.
Above Rs.1,00,000/- - 10% to 15%.

RATE OF INTEREST AND PROCESSING CHARGES

  •   To know rate of interest, please click here

Repayment

  • Plantation -- 4 years
  • Equipment -- 3 years
  • Rearing House -- 5 years
  • Reeling unit -- 7 years

Security

  • Up to Rs. 1,00,000/- DPN, Hypothecation of assets created and crops.
  • Over Rs. 1,00,000/- DPN, Hypothecation of assets created and crops, mortgage of land &/or third party guarantee.

LIST OF DOCUMENTS

  • Up to Rs. 1,00,000/- DPN, Hypothecation of assets created and crops.
  • Over Rs. 1,00,000/- DPN, Hypothecation of assets created and crops, mortgage of land &/or third party guarantee.
