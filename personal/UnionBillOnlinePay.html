  • 1. What is bill payment?

    Ans. It includes utility bill payments like electricity bill, telephone bill, insurance premium payments, online railway/Air ticket bookings etc.

  • 2. Which bills can be paid through bill payment?

    Ans. Please check the biller list provided in our site. http://www.unionbankofindia.co.in/internet_billpay.aspx

  • 3. In case of failed bill payment what should I do?

    Ans. Please call our 24x7 call center and register your complaint by informing the necessary details

  • 4. How many days it takes to revert back the failed transaction amount?

    Ans. The refund of failed transaction amount depends on the biller, however usually it takes 7 to 14 days.

  • 5. whether any registration is required for using this service

    Ans. No registration is required with internet banking. However biller like IRCTC, LIC etc. may ask for the registration

  • 6. Whom should I approach to clarify my queries?

    Ans. Please call our 24x7 call center to know the procedure and other details. You may also email your failed/ duplicate transaction details to etax@unionbankofindia.com for resolution of an issue.
