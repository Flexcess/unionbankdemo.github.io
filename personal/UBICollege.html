AROUND THE COLLEGE

Around the College : Union Bank of India Staff College, Bannerghatta, Bengaluru

The Staff College campus is beautifully maintained and has a jogging track, gymnasium, badminton courts, swimming pool, boating pond, and mango / chiku / coconut orchards as well as a farm with dairy cattle and sheep.

Games rooms are available for Table tennis, Billiards, Carrom etc. There is a well stocked Library with very good support from the Librarian, and the campus is Wifi enabled so that the internet can be accessed from any point. A technically advanced Auditorium is used for special functions.

There are Staff quarters on campus, and Hostel / Executive Hostel for those undergoing training. The Guest House is used by Top Management and visiting Dignitaries.
